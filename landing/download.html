After Install

You'll be live in minutes.

1
Point it at your vault
Run bash scripts/setup.sh and enter the path to your music folder. Paperweight scans it immediately — no upload required.
2
Open your Studio dashboard
Visit localhost:3000/#dashboard and unlock Studio with your token. Configure your station name, set up your schedule, and toggle tracks live.
3
Share your station
Enable the Cloudflare Tunnel from Studio and your station gets a public URL — no port-forwarding, no static IP. Issue access tokens to let listeners in.
