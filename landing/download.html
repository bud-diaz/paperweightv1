What Happens Next

From install to live in minutes.

1
setup.sh configures everything
bash scripts/setup.sh walks you through your station name, public slug (slug.paperweighthq.com), vault path, and optional Cloudflare Tunnel token. It prints your dashboard token at the end — save it.
2
Preflight checks your environment
npm run preflight validates Node.js, FFmpeg, your vault path, and your .env before you go live. If anything is missing it tells you exactly what to fix.
3
Start up and open Studio
Run npm start, then open localhost:3000/#dashboard and unlock with your token. Drop files in your vault, hit Go Live, and your station is on air.
